When do courses start and end?

Course start and end dates are determined by the course provider. That said, we work closely with edX and our University partners to optimize alignment between our University partners' academic calendar and the start and end date of select course offerings.

Will our students have to pay an additional cost for their certificates once they've completed a course?

No.

What happens is a student does not complete their assigned course within the allotted timeframe?

To incentivize timely course completion, we strongly encourage partners to offer our provided courses as co-requisites alongside existing curricula offerings. A student who fails to complete a course within the allotted time in this scenario will be subject to institutional policies regarding this course outcome.

What will it cost our institution to purchase a package of courses?

The price of courses will vary based on a variety of factors including whether or not it is a certificate granting course. For this reason, pricing will be based on the suite of courses you choose. Regardless of your selection, as a part of our social impact mission, TaSA is committed to ensuring up to a 40% discount (to university partners) on the actual value of edX provided courses.

Do I have to pay to start a company account on TaSA?

No. You can start a company account and create a company profile on TaSA for free. We also offer premium membership accounts.

How will signing up for a TaSA account benefit my company?

Benefits of a TaSA account to your company are as follows:

  • Human Resources: Access to the largest pool of high skill job seekers on the continent.
  • Branding: Increase your brand visibility to the millions of prospective TaSA members in Africa and abroad much quicker and for far less than it costs to create a website.
  • Marketing: Market your products to a vast potential consumer base of continental and diaspora Africans.
How is TaSA different from LinkedIn or other professional networking or job search sites?

TaSA (Talent Source Africa) aims to cultivate the largest network of African and diaspora talent in the world. So whether you're looking to hire, market to, connect with, learn from, or simply breathe with Black and African people on the continent and in the diaspora, look no further than TaSA.

How much does it cost to post a job opening on TaSA?

NO, The cost of job postings on TaSA are variable with factors such as length (how long you want it to be up) of posting; the level of targeting (do you want it to go to a particular audience) and industry impacting pricing.
